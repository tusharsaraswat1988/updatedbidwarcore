The reserve purse and squad protection backend logic appears implemented, but the frontend visibility and operator clarity are still incomplete.

Current issue:
operators and owners cannot clearly understand:
- why reserve purse exists
- how many mandatory slots remain
- what their actual spendable purse is
- why bidding becomes restricted

This feature must become highly visible and understandable across the entire UI.

REQUIRED FRONTEND IMPROVEMENTS
---------------------------------------------------

Add a clearly visible "Squad Rules" section in tournament settings.

Fields:
- Minimum Squad Size
- Maximum Squad Size
- Reserve Purse Protection
- Dynamic Lowest Base Price Logic

This should appear in:
- tournament creation
- tournament edit
- command hub settings

TEAM CARD IMPROVEMENTS

- Total Purse
- Remaining Purse
- Reserved Purse
- Spendable Purse

- Mandatory Slots Remaining

Purse: ₹82.5L

Squad: 4 / 12 Min
