Implement a professional Broadcast Audio System for live auctions.

This system must be designed for:
- low latency
- premium live auction atmosphere
- future broadcast expansion

IMPORTANT:
Audio playback must happen from the LIVE LED DISPLAY client only.

Do NOT depend on backend-triggered streaming audio playback.

---------------------------------------------------
REQUIRED AUDIO TYPES
---------------------------------------------------

1. Countdown Sound
2. Sold Sound

---------------------------------------------------
COUNTDOWN SOUND
---------------------------------------------------

Purpose:
Create tension during the last 5 seconds of bidding.

Behavior:
- only trigger during last 5 seconds
- subtle and professional
- synchronized with visible timer
- no loud arcade/game sounds

Recommended style:
- digital tick
- auction pulse
- sports countdown tone

---------------------------------------------------
SOLD SOUND
---------------------------------------------------

Purpose:
Celebrate successful player sale.

Behavior:
- trigger only after confirmed SOLD state
- sync with sold animation/stamp
- premium sports-auction feel

Future-ready:
support longer sold stingers/music clips.

---------------------------------------------------
TOURNAMENT SETTINGS INTEGRATION
---------------------------------------------------

Inside:
Tournament Settings
→ Broadcast
→ Audio

Add separate configurable sections:

1. Countdown Sound
2. Sold Sound

Each section should support:
- enable/disable
- audio file selection
- volume
- preview playback

---------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
---------------------------------------------------

Default audio files may exist initially,
but the admin must be able to replace them later.

Do NOT hardcode permanent audio assets.

---------------------------------------------------
PRELOAD REQUIREMENT
---------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT:

Audio files must preload locally on the LIVE LED DISPLAY client when the broadcast screen loads.

Do NOT fetch audio repeatedly from backend during live bidding.

Reason:
- prevent latency
- prevent playback lag
- avoid network delays
- ensure instant playback

---------------------------------------------------
AUDIO MANAGER REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------

Implement proper audio state management.

Requirements:
- prevent overlapping sounds
- stop countdown when sold sound triggers
- avoid duplicate playback
- handle rapid auction transitions cleanly

---------------------------------------------------
LIVE DISPLAY RESPONSIBILITY
---------------------------------------------------

ONLY the broadcast/LED display should play sounds.

Operator panel and owner panel must NOT auto-play auction sounds.

This prevents:
- audio echo
- multiple device playback
- synchronization issues

---------------------------------------------------
FUTURE EXPANSION SUPPORT
---------------------------------------------------

Architecture should support future additions:
- IPL-style sold music
- sponsor stingers
- category-specific sounds
- franchise themes
- retained player music
- championship moments

---------------------------------------------------
BROADCAST CONTROL
---------------------------------------------------

Add:
- Broadcast Audio Enable/Disable
- Master Volume
- Emergency Mute

---------------------------------------------------
ENGINEERING REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------

- preserve live auction performance
- no blocking audio operations
- preload assets efficiently
- low-latency playback
- lightweight implementation
- smooth mobile/browser compatibility

This should feel like a premium live sports auction broadcast system.
